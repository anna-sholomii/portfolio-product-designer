The principles of my work
1

I treat the brief as the starting point, not the answer.

The question you start with determines more about the outcome than the solution you build.

2

Research is a habit, not a phase.

What people say they do and what they actually do are rarely the same thing.

3

I design for how people actually decide - not how they should.

I design for someone distracted, uncertain, deciding in under 90 seconds.

4

The goal is removing confusion, not adding features.

The measure of a good interface is how little the user has to figure out.
